Crochet Care Guide 🧼

Keep your handmade cuties soft and sweet for years to come!

🧺

Washing

Hand wash gently in cold water using a mild detergent. Avoid harsh scrubbing which can cause pilling.

🌬️

Drying

Lay flat on a clean towel to air dry. Do not hang, as the weight of the water can stretch the stitches!

✂️

Fuzzies

If your item gets "pills" or fuzzy over time, you can carefully trim them with small scissors or a fabric shaver.

🚫

The Don'ts

Never put your crochet items in the dryer or use bleach. The heat can melt synthetic fibers or shrink cotton.
